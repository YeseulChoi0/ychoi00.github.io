Hot Chocolate Recipe

Description

The best hot chocolate you will ever make in your life with an incredible amount of chocolate. For all chocolate lovers who practically eat their hot chocolate.

Ingredients

  • 3 heaping tablespoon of Nutella
  • 2 big bars of chocolate (your choice)
  • 2 cups of whole milk
  • Marshmallows, whipped cream, more chocolate or all of the above, for topping

Directions

  1. In a large pot, melt the chocolate completely on low heat
  2. Once the chocolate is completely liquidified, stirr in Nutella in big chunks
  3. Stirr in your milk slowly and turn your heat off
  4. Pour your hot chocolate in a large cup
  5. Top with marshmallows, whipped cream and more chocolate
